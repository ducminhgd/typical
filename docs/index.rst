Getting Started
===============

``typical`` is a Python-only package `hosted on PyPI <https://pypi.org/project/typical/>`_.

.. code-block:: console

   $ pip install typical

The next three steps should bring you up and running in no time:

- `overview` will show you a simple example of ``typical`` in action and introduce you
  to its philosophy.
- `usage` will give you a quick look at the high-level API.
- `extras` will give you a comprehensive tour of ``typical``'s features.
- Finally `why` gives you a rundown of potential alternatives and why we think
  ``typical`` is superior.
